Phishing Awareness

Phishing is the most common way attackers steal credentials and money. Learn how to spot phishing messages, protect personal information, and act fast if you suspect a scam.

Short, practical advice — not tech jargon. Designed for everyone.

How it works Prevention tips & videos

Quick 5-second check

  • Look at sender: Is the domain suspicious?
  • Check links: Hover (desktop) or inspect link before tapping.
  • Urgency: Scammers push panic—slow down.
  • Attachments: Unexpected files are risky.

Why this matters

Phishing attacks target individuals and companies. A single click can expose passwords, bank details, and private data. Small habits prevent most scams.
